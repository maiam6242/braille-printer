Ready to turn your document into some beautiful braille? Follow the instructions below to get started with the Portabraille Printer!

First, turn the printer on by flipping the power switch.

 

(insert location of power switch)

Next, you’ll need to you’ll need to upload a file to the printer.

 

You can do this manually or wirelessly. If you’d like to manually upload your file, insert a flash drive with the file into the printer USB port (insert location), then use the [PLAY/PAUSE] button on the interface to toggle through and select your file. If you’d like to wirelessly upload your file, connect your device to the Portabraille Wi-Fi network, then use the Portabraille Portal interface to upload and submit your file.

Almost ready! Prepare your paper and check the READY TO PRINT indicator.

 

The Portabraille Printer takes in one sheet of paper at a time, so you’ll need to manually feed in each sheet by lining it up in the paper tray [insert location of paper tray]. Additionally, before you print, make sure to get visual or aural confirmation from the READY TO PRINT indicator!

Finally, hit the PLAY button to get your print started!

 

Get ready for some beautiful braille! However, if there’s a problem with your print, hit the CANCEL button to completely stop it, or the PLAY/PAUSE button to pause and then restart the print.

 

The Portabraille Printer also has an ERROR indicator LED and a READY TO PRINT indicator LED, located in the top corners of the interface. These indicators play noises, but if you’d prefer a more quiet printing experience, change the toggle switch at the bottom of the interface to SOUND OFF instead of SOUND ON.
